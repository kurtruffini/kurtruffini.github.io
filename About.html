Hello,

I'm Kurt, a fourth year mechanical engineering student at The University of British Columbia. My passions include but are not limited to manufacturing and R&D, with a strong focus in sustainability. Hobbies

I wanted to improve my hands on skills so I purchased a project car to fix. Some things I worked on included: installing new control arms, tie rods, sway bar links, rear bearings, cold air intake, wheel spacers, brake pads and rotors, sound system, serpentine belt, brake light sensor and O2 sensor. 

I thoroughly enjoy an active lifestyle and one of my favourite ways to spend my time off during the summer months is to get on the water and wake surf. I also enjoy mountain biking, golfing, tennis and hiking.
During the winter months, I take advantage of the snow and go snowboarding.

I have participated in various competitive sports growing up including volleyball, basketball, ball hockey and ultimate frisbee. My volleyball team placed top 4 in the province of BC numerous times, where I was also awarded the Provincial Allstar in both 2015 and 2017. 

I have played the piano since the age of 4, completing levels 1-9 in The Royal Conservatory of Music
