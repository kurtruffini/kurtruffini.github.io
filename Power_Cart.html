Power Cart

I fully assembled a 10,000 psi hydraulic power cart which included installing hydraulic & pneumatic hoses/fittings, cable reels, a hydraulic pump, FRL unit, hydraulic filter and control valves. I also used a hand tube bender to bend stainless steel pneumatic air tubes.

A custom bracket had to be designed using SolidWorks to mount the FRL unit to the power cart.
