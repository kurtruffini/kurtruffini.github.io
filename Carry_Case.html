Carry Case

I had the opportunity to design a carry case for a haul truck wheel inspection system that I developed. The carry case featured four distinct foam layers. This design allowed for easy stacking and maximized space utilization, resulting in a more compact and efficient packing solution. Through this project, I gained valuable experience in designing effective and efficient packaging solutions.
